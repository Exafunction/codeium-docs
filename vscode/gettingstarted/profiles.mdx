## User Analytics

User analytics are available for viewing and sharing on your own [profile](https://codeium.com/profile) page!

See your completion stats, [refer](https://codeium.com/referral) your friends, look into your language breakdown, and more!

<Tip>Fun Fact: the more friends you refer, the higher up you move in our premium model [waitlist](https://codeium.com/waitlist/gpt-4)! Read more on our [Models](/vscode/chat/models) page.</Tip>

You can even unlock achievement badges just by using the Codeium in your daily work flow. 

## Team Analytics

<Note>You will need team admin privileges in order to view the following team links.</Note>

Codeium makes managing your team easy from one [dashboard](https://codeium.com/team/). 

Team leads and managers can also see an aggregate of all of their team members' analytics! Click [here](https://codeium.com/team/analytics) to view.

We further extrapolate this out for you to truly see the value that Codeium is providing to your team, such as Percent of Code Written and Time/$ Saved.
