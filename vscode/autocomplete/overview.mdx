Codeium Autocomplete is:
- the only autocomplete tool that can Fill-In-the_Middle (FIM). It takes in context from before and after the cursor in order to make suggestions.

- able to make inline and multi-line suggestions and edits.

- able to remember your previous suggestions and changes to your code. 

All users receive unlimited autocomplete suggestions!

## Keyboard Shortcuts

To accept an autocompletion suggestion: `tab`

To accept an autocompletion suggestion one word at a time: `⌘` + `→`

Next Completion: `⌥` + `]` Previous Completion: `⌥` + `[` 

Trigger Completion: `⌥` + `\`

## Toggle

Click the Codeium button in the bottom right and you will see the button to toggle it on and off.
